Name:DeathCertificate
Title:VRDR Death Certificate
Status:Draft as of 2020-05-09
Definition:

The Death Certificate profile is the body of the death certificate document. It builds upon the FHIR Composition resource.

Publisher:HL7 Public Health Working Group
